Info:

Sometimes, being able to externalize your thoughts can have a positive impact on your state of mind, your well-being, and your ability to thrive in the face of life's adventures. This page provides a space for you to do that.

The idea is to take the healthy practice of journaling one tiny step further by making it a bit more interactive. After writing, you can choose to process your entry and receive a basic insight and sentiment (mood) analysis assessment based on some simple statistical analysis on your journal-entry.

Of course, you should receive the assessment with little more credibility than you would a fortune cookie. This in no way, takes the place of the immensely restorative benefits of visiting a mental health professional. If you do need someone to talk to - don't hesitate to reach out.

In the text box, just go ahead, and type away. Type away even if it doesn't make sense, or if you spell something wrong. No judgement here, I promise! Anything works. As soon as you refresh the page, the text goes away - none of it's stored.
