Info:

I'm a big fan of good movies. This page showcases scenes from three of my favorites.

The idea is for you to experience the different stories created through mixing and matching 3-minute clips from each of the three components (scene, song, and sound).

The sounds are more uniquely tied to clips but the soundtracks should be more freely interchangeable. See the nuances that each different soundtrack/sound brings to the scene.

I don't own any of the featured video clips or soundtracks. The sound clips are royalty free. This is purely for fun.

Happy mixing!
