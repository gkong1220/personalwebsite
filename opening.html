About

This was my final project for MUSC 320: Orchestration and Arranging - a four-part ensemble arrangement of Philip Glass' brilliant piano piece.

The piece follows a slow-moving, simply-constructed harmonic progression that is carried on dutifully in this arrangement by the inner voices (viola, cello) accompanied by the underlying/overlying voices of baritone and flute respectively. This orchestration allows for a dissected look at how each part functions to create beautiful, homegenous sound in Minmalist style.

Credit to Dr. Adler, professor of the course for guidance, direciton on writing this arrangement and conducting of first recording. And another thank you to Mariana, David, Cecilia, and Jonathan who contributed their instrumentals/voices to the first demo recording.
