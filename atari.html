Info:

A classic - designed for left-handed and right-handed enthusiasts.

Move your paddle with either "W"/"S" keys or the Up/Down keys - whichever side you prefer.

"W"/"S" will control the left paddle and Up/Down keys control the right paddle. The left and right arrow keys set paddle side at the beginning of the game and resets only on page refresh.

Best of luck! Play to 21.
