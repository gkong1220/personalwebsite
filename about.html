Hi! Welcome to the website. Just a little bit about what you might find here...

I've always been fascinated with user-centered design. Here, I think I've finally found an outlet to explore that.

I care immensely about clean, functional interfaces that people find a joy to work with/use. More than just a portfolio to showcase my work, I want people to be able to interact with my work. I hope people get to actually create something with their interactions here.

In this bundled, jumbled collection of stuff, you'll find games to pass the time, opportunities to make music, and a platform to create and experience an iconic movie moment from new perspectives. This web project is all about exploration - so, go ahead, explore!

At the end of the day, I hope my work speaks for itself.
